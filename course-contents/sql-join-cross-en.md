Following cross join sql in Chinook, cartesian multiply Genre and MediaType tables and bring us 125 rows.

```sql
SELECT  G.Name,M.Name
FROM Genre G CROSS JOIN MediaType M;
```

- Genre 25 rows
- MediaType 5 rows
